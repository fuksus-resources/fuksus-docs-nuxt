## Before installing

::warning
  Make sure your server's artifacts are updated to 4752 or higher!
::

## Installation steps

::steps{level="4"}
  #### Step 1: Download resource from keymaster
  
  Go to your [keymaster](https://keymaster.fivem.net/) page and download `fuksus-crafting`.
  
  #### Step 2: Place resource in your resources folder
  
  Grab your `fuksus-crafting.pack.zip` and extract it in your `server/resources/` folder.

  #### Step 3: Configure your framework

  Check out the bridge folders and configure your framework.
    > These frameworks are preconfigured:
    - ESX
    - QBCore
    - QBox

  #### Step 4: Enjoy your new resource

  Enjoy your new high quality resource!
::
